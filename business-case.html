02 / The Problem

The Status Quo Is Unsustainable

Manual safety signage auditing is slow, inconsistent, and expensive.

The Manual Process

  • Handwritten field notes on clipboards or generic forms
  • Manual GPS recording — often skipped or inaccurate
  • Photos taken separately, manually matched to notes later
  • 15–30 minutes per sign including office write-up
  • Separate office session to compile the final report
  • AS 1319 clause references looked up manually — often inconsistent or missing

The Consequences

  • Audit reports take days to finalise after site visits
  • Junior officers produce inconsistent quality — senior review required
  • No standardised evidence trail — hard to defend in compliance disputes
  • Photo-to-finding linkage breaks down on large sites
  • Repeat audits cannot be reliably compared to previous results
  • High labour cost: a 200-sign site consumes 67–133 hours

09 / Risk & Mitigation

Risk Assessment

Identified risks and their mitigations.

Risk Mitigation
Data privacy All data stored locally on-device in IndexedDB — no cloud upload required. Photos and audit data remain under WSP control.
Vendor dependency App works fully offline. All data exportable via CSV at any time. No vendor lock-in on audit records.
AI accuracy AI provides initial assessment only — the auditor always reviews and can override every finding. Dual audit trail preserves both versions.
App discontinuation All data is portable via CSV export. No proprietary formats. Audit records can be migrated to any system.
Standard changes AS 1319-1994 has been unchanged since 1994 (reconfirmed 2018). Low probability of breaking changes. App updates can accommodate revisions.
