Sizing Guide (Clause 3.4.2)

Good lighting, prominent position:

  • Symbolic sign size: 15 mm per metre of viewing distance
  • Upper case letters: 5 mm per metre
  • Lower case letters: 4 mm per metre

Poor lighting / less conspicuous: Increase by 50%

Key Installation Requirements (Section 4)

  • Signs must be visible, legible, and attract attention (4.2.1)
  • Mount near observer line of sight: ~1500mm above floor ±5° (4.2.2)
  • Regulatory/hazard signs: allow ample reaction time (4.2.3)
  • Do not place on moveable objects that void purpose (4.2.4)
  • Consider illumination where lighting is poor (4.2.5)
  • Avoid excessive sign clustering that reduces comprehension (4.2.6)
  • Remove signs when information is no longer relevant (4.1)
  • Signs must not create a hazard themselves (4.1)
